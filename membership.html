Membership

We are happy to offer you the option of a full membership or pay as you go. Membership includes full access to all gym facilities and aerobics classes every day. For full memberships there is a small initial joining fee of €25 for adults and €15 for junior members. Short term memberships (3 months) have a €15 joining fee. This is a once off non refundable registration fee.
